# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

boostCX is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone — regardless of experience level, background, identity, or role.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and professional in all interactions.
- Give and accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the team.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Disruptive, trolling, or deliberately hostile conduct.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the repository maintainers or organization admins. Reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly and confidentially.

Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, commits, or contributions that violate this code, and to restrict access for repeated violations.
